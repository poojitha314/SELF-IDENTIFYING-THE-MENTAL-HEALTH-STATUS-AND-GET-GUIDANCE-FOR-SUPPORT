Aromatherapy

Lavender

Lavender essential oil is known for its calming and relaxing properties. It can help reduce stress, anxiety, and promote better sleep.

Mixing Ratio: For relaxation, mix 3-5 drops of lavender essential oil with 1 ounce (30 mL) of carrier oil (such as coconut oil) for massage or add a few drops to a diffuser with water.

Peppermint

Peppermint essential oil has invigorating and refreshing properties. It can help relieve headaches, improve concentration, and boost energy levels.

Mixing Ratio: For headache relief, mix 2-3 drops of peppermint essential oil with 1 teaspoon (5 mL) of carrier oil and apply to temples or back of neck. For energy boost, add a few drops to a diffuser with water.

Lemon

Lemon essential oil has a fresh and uplifting scent. It can help improve mood, increase focus, and promote feelings of cleanliness and purification.

Mixing Ratio: For mood enhancement, add 3-4 drops of lemon essential oil to a diffuser with water. For cleaning, mix 10-20 drops with 1 cup (240 mL) of water in a spray bottle.
